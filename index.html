Contribute to HypeTonic

Want to write for HypeTonic? Follow the directions below!

  • 1 Make a Medium Account
  • 2 Request to be a Writer
  • 3 Write a Really Cool Article
  • 4 Submit for Us to Review
  • 5 See Your Published Masterpiece!

Because we believe in the idea of open source and love the open source community, anybody can write for HypeTonic. We made the process so easy! To get started, you must first have a Medium account. Next, shoot us an email requesting to be a writer so that we can feature your articles in the publication. If you don't want to email us, you can request to become a writer via Twitter too! Once we add you as a writer on Medium, you can write an article about anything related to open source. Now, use Medium to submit the article to us. If we deem article appropriate for HypeTonic, we'll have your work published!
